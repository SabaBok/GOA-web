About Goal Oriented Academy

Goal Oriented Academy (GOA) is a leading programming school in Georgia, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of tech innovators. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology.

At GOA, we believe in a hands-on, project-based learning approach that empowers students to think critically, solve problems, and create meaningful solutions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced programmer, our courses are designed to meet your needs.

Nika Keshelava

Founder & Lead Instructor

Nika Keshelava founded the Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) to provide personalized, practical programming education in Georgia. Motivated by the shortcomings of traditional education, he created a space where students could gain technical skills alongside leadership, creativity, and critical thinking.GOA serves various age groups with a hybrid learning model that combines online lessons with real-world projects and community events. Under Keshelava’s leadership, the academy emphasizes mentorship, teamwork, and personal growth.His mission is to build a community of empowered, goal-driven individuals who are ready to make a positive impact in the tech industry and society.
