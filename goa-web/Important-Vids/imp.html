Goal Oriented Academy

Escape From The Matrix

Goal Oriented Academy is a Georgian programming course dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. Their core values include a commitment to practical, hands-on learning, empowering students to think critically and solve real-world problems. They emphasize collaboration, continuous improvement, and a passion for technology, ensuring that every student is equipped with the skills and mindset needed to succeed in the ever-evolving tech industry.

Hackathon Competition and The Lecture

Regularly in GOA a friencly competition comes along with the name of "Hackathon". On this day at first one of the first mentors who even taught the founder Nika Keshelava comes along and gives a lecture about what programing is, what they should expect, give advices and answer the questions asked by the students who came. later on after the lecture ends begings the competition in which students are given a certain ammount of time to solve complex programming quizes and if they volunteer to also compete in a pushup challenge all this to see at the end who is gets in the top 3, the top 3 get the following prizes in this order: 300,200,100 lari, however if the students also wins in a pushup competition he will also get exra prize for the effort
